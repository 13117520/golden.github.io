Our Tailoring Services

From everyday wardrobe fixes to complex wedding dress resizing, our Stockport studio provides precise, high-quality garment care for every occasion.

Everyday Clothes Alterations

Don't throw away your favourite garments. We expertly handle jeans shortening, trouser tapering, zip replacements, and seam repairs. Whether it's taking in a dress or fixing a torn jacket, our daily clothing alterations breathe new life into your wardrobe.

Suit Tailoring & Resizing

A well-fitted suit makes all the difference. We provide professional suit tailoring for men and women. From adjusting jacket sleeves and taking in the waist, to tapering trousers for a modern fit, we ensure you look sharp for business, interviews, or special events.

Bridal & Evening Wear

Your big day requires absolute perfection. Our experienced tailors specialise in delicate wedding dress alterations, bridesmaid dress fitting, and prom dress resizing. We work meticulously with lace, silk, and beadwork to achieve a flawless, custom fit.
